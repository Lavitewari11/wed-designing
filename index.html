Why Choose Razorpay?

Secure Payments

PCI-DSS compliant system ensuring safe transactions. Razorpay is chosen because it provides a fast, secure, and reliable payment solution that is easy to integrate for businesses and developers. It supports multiple payment methods such as UPI, debit and credit cards, net banking, wallets, and EMI, allowing customers to pay conveniently. Razorpay follows high security standards like PCI-DSS compliance and data encryption, which ensures safe and fraud-free transactions. It also offers quick settlement cycles and a user-friendly dashboard for real-time transaction tracking and reporting. Due to its strong customer support and wide adoption by startups, small businesses, and large enterprises in India, Razorpay is a trusted and efficient payment gateway.

Quick Settlements

Receive payments instantly with automated settlements. Quick settlement in Razorpay refers to the fast transfer of collected payments from the merchant’s Razorpay account to their bank account. Razorpay enables businesses to receive their funds within a short time period, often as early as the next working day, which helps maintain smooth cash flow and financial stability. This feature is especially beneficial for startups and small businesses, as it reduces waiting time for funds and allows them to manage expenses, salaries, and operations efficiently. By providing faster and reliable settlements, Razorpay helps businesses focus more on growth rather than payment delays.

Easy Integration

Supports websites, apps, and POS devices. Easy integration in Razorpay means that businesses can quickly and smoothly add online payment functionality to their websites or applications without complexity. Razorpay provides well-documented APIs, SDKs, and ready-made plugins for popular platforms, making the integration process simple even for beginners. Developers can easily connect Razorpay with websites built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, or other technologies. This reduces development time, minimizes technical errors, and allows businesses to start accepting payments faster, making Razorpay a convenient choice for online payment integration.

Business Banking Solutions

RazorpayX

Smart bank accounts for modern businesses.RazorpayX is a next-generation business banking suite that provides businesses with a digital banking experience including current accounts that can be opened online and used instantly. With RazorpayX, businesses can manage money movements, approve multi-user workflows, schedule payouts, and view real-time financial insights—all from one place. This modern banking experience eliminates many traditional banking hassles like paperwork, long queues, and slow processing.

Payouts

Automated vendor, salary, and customer payouts.The Payouts system under RazorpayX allows businesses to send money easily to their vendors, employees, or customers. It supports multiple payment methods like IMPS, NEFT, RTGS, and UPI, and even enables payouts to cards—all with built-in automation and bulk processing. Businesses can add payout beneficiaries instantly, schedule payments ahead of time, or use APIs for automated payout workflows. This makes paying multiple parties fast, reliable, and scalable, especially for large businesses handling hundreds or thousands of transactions. Razorpay

Current Accounts

Reliable banking with higher transaction limits.A Current Account powered by RazorpayX functions like a traditional business bank account but with digital ease and automation. Businesses can open a current account online, enjoy instant payouts, and take advantage of detailed dashboards that show cash flow, transaction history, and accounting insights. RazorpayX current accounts are integrated with business tools and support features like bulk payouts, account verification, payroll, tax payments, and third-party software integrations—all designed to replace multiple disconnected financial tools with a single digital platform. Simple & Transparent Pricing

Standard Plan

2% per successful transaction The Standard Plan is ideal for small and new businesses. It charges a fixed fee per successful transaction and supports common payment methods such as UPI, debit cards, credit cards, and net banking. There are no hidden charges, making it a cost-effective and reliable option.

Premium Plan

1.5% + advanced features The Premium Plan is designed for growing businesses. It offers lower transaction fees along with advanced features such as faster settlements, higher payment success rates, and priority customer support. This plan is suitable for businesses with higher transaction volumes.

Custom Plan

Suited for enterprise clients The Custom Plan is meant for large and enterprise-level businesses. In this plan, pricing and features are customized according to business requirements. It includes special discounts, dedicated support, custom settlements, and advanced reporting features.
